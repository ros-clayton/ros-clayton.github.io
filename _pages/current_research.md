## Working Papers

**[The impact of early grade repetition on test scores: Evidence from a regression discontinuity design in South Africa](https://resep.sun.ac.za/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/WP06_25_Ros-Clayton-Impact-of-early-grade-repetition.pdf)**

Resep working paper, 2025.

Uses administrative data for six provinces and an RD design to estimate the causal impact of Grade 1 and Grade 4 repetition on later achievement.

---

**[Linguistic interdependence? Foundation Phase mastery in home language as a predictor of grade 4 repetition and EFAL marks](https://resep.sun.ac.za/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/WP02_25-Linguistic-Interdependence-Ros-Clayton.pdf)**

Resep working paper, 2025.

Studies the relationship between Foundation Phase language proficiency and later academic outcomes.

---

**[From Grade 7 arithmetic to matric: A quantitative evaluation of the Numeric afterschool maths program](https://numeric.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Numeric-Learner-Longitudinal-Study-full-report.pdf)**

Evaluation report, 2024.

Randomised evaluation of an after-school programme for primary school learners in South Africa, estimating causal impacts on academic outcomes up to high school completion.
